Senior Product Manager Assignment - Campaign Engine

Executive Summary

A modular, rule based campaign engine that allows operators to design, automate and optimize player promotions without engineering intervention. The campaign engine will allow operators to create flexible, rule driven campaigns by

  • listen to player behaviour triggers (internally or externally)
  • determining eligibility based on specific player segments
  • giving out rewards according to specified guidelines to achieve positive ROI

Problem Statement

Campaign systems across iGaming platforms are largely aimed at acquiring new players, retention and engagement. As the industry evolves, so does the demand for more innovative campaign solutions which often outpace the product’s ability to adapt in time. This leads to pain points such as:

  • the need to manage bespoke campaigns manually (e.g. determining player eligibility via manual reports, and giving bonuses manually) until the product is able to catch up
  • multiple CRM integration requests to address product gaps in different parts of the campaign life cycle (e.g. not enough segment options in the campaign engine)
  • difficulty in knowing for sure whether the campaign will perform well enough before the operator commits to it
  • bonus abusers becoming more creative with their methods, outsmarting the system
  • irrelevant promotions given to players
  • poor communication on expectations and reward mechanics

Product Vision

A next gen, UX aware campaign engine which allows enough flexibility to design, review, launch and measure promotional campaigns tailored to specific markets and jurisdictions, generating ROI for the operator’s casino with ease.

Proposed Design

  1. The campaign engine shall be a wizard style which splits the campaign creation in 3 simple steps:
    1. Basic Campaign Setup
    1. Player Eligibility
    1. Reward Config & Distribution
  1. Each step shall represent the building blocks of the campaign which contains specific configuration details that the campaign manager is expected to define in the day to day operations

  1. Player Eligibility can be defined in two modes:
    1. Manual Assignment
    1. Targeted Assignment (through player segmentation)
  1. In Manual Assignment mode, all segmentation criteria is hidden, since this promotion will be assigned manually through the backoffice once it is saved
  1. In Targeted mode, further segmentation options are available which are
    1. Import Player List, either by
      1. csv upload
      1. webhook
    1. Player Segments
  1. Player segments shall be calculated on these events
    1. Login
    1. Deposit
  1. Player Segments shall be logically ANDed together
  1. If imported lists are available, these are also to be logically ANDed with the rest of the segments
  1. The whole step of Player Eligibility may be left untouched, which effectively means that all players are targeted (Open to all).

  1. It is assumed that the data warehouse is still in its infancy and has yet to create more advanced data aggregations for fine grained player segmentation
